Why I Built This

I'm Idan. I went bald at 25. This is the product I wish someone had sold me honestly.

When my hair started thinning, I tried everything to hide it — hairstyles, hats, even a glued-on hair system that itched, sweated, and drove me crazy for a month at a time before I ripped it off and accepted the bald life.

Then came years of razor shaving. Every day or two: cold water on my scalp in winter, foam everywhere, nicks on sensitive skin, missed patches behind my head, and a bathroom that looked like a crime scene. It was so exhausting I'd only shave once or twice a week — and look unkempt the rest of the time.

Then I finally found an electric head shaver that actually worked. Two minutes, dry, no foam, hair collected inside the shaver. I loved it.

Until I discovered they'd quietly signed me up for a blade subscription I never asked for — and couldn't cancel.

I checked. Almost every big brand in this space plays the same game. That made me angry enough to start my own.

"Mensch" means a decent, honest person. That's the whole brand: a great head shaver, one fair price, no fine print. We're bald, brothers — haven't we suffered enough? 😄
